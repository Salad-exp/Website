Paid?

Salad Executor is completely free and keyless. It is powered by CeleryAPI, but we modified it to support more scripts.

Malware?

Salad Executor is not protected with any anti-reverse engineering tool, allowing users to view its source code using debugging tools such as DnSpy. Furthermore, users who claim that the software is malicious often do not respond when requested to provide evidence.
